## Why Use Producer Chat vs "Generate Story"?

### "Generate Story" Button (Classic Pipeline)
- **Fixed workflow**: Every project goes through the same steps
- **No customization**: 15-second TikTok = feature film treatment
- **Fast**: Just click and go
- **Good for**: Standard narrative projects with no special needs

### "Chat with Producer" Button (META-ORCHESTRATOR)
- **Adaptive workflow**: Different projects get different treatment
- **Context-aware**: Understands your goals and constraints
- **Conversational**: You explain what you need, it adapts
- **Good for**:
  - Character tests
  - Quick experiments
  - Client deliverables (needs polish)
  - Time-constrained projects
  - Non-standard workflows

## Troubleshooting

### "I don't see the Chat with Producer button"

**Check:**
1. Do you have a project open? (Not on Dashboard)
2. Is your project idea filled in? (Button is disabled if blank)
3. Have you already generated a structure? (Producer only shows BEFORE first generation)

**Solution:**
- Create a new project
- Write your idea in the brief composer
- Look for the purple "Chat with Producer" button next to "Generate Story"

### "The chat disappeared"

The chat auto-closes when workflow starts executing. This is intentional - you'll see progress indicators instead.

**To re-open:** Click the floating purple brain icon (bottom-right)

### "Producer isn't understanding my request"

Be more specific:
- âŒ "Make something cool"
- âœ… "Quick 30-second character test, rough quality"

Include:
- **Length/scope**: "30 seconds", "3 minutes", "10+ shots"
- **Quality level**: "rough draft", "polished", "client-ready"
- **Purpose**: "testing an idea", "client deliverable", "festival submission"
